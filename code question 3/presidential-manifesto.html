Our Vision & Mission for Uganda

VISION 2031

"A Transformed Ugandan Society from a Peasant to a Modern and Prosperous Country within 30 Years"

Our Core Mission

To continue building a unified, democratic, and prosperous Uganda where every citizen enjoys equal opportunities, dignity, and access to quality services while preserving our cultural heritage and national sovereignty.

Fundamental Principles

  • National Unity: Fostering harmony among all Ugandans regardless of tribe, religion, or region
  • Economic Transformation: Moving from subsistence to commercial agriculture and industrialization
  • Good Governance: Strengthening democratic institutions and the rule of law
  • Social Justice: Ensuring equitable distribution of national resources and opportunities
  • Pan-Africanism: Promoting African unity and integration

Economic Development Strategy

Our economic vision centers on transforming Uganda from a predominantly agricultural economy to a modern, diversified, and industrialized nation by 2031.

Key Economic Pillars

Industrialization Drive

  • Establish industrial parks in every region
  • Promote value addition to agricultural products
  • Attract foreign direct investment in manufacturing
  • Support local entrepreneurs and SMEs

Employment Creation

Generate 2.5 million jobs in the next 5 years through:

  • Youth-focused entrepreneurship programs
  • Skills development and technical training
  • Support for cooperative societies
  • Expansion of the service sector

Regional Integration

Leverage Uganda's strategic position to become the commercial hub of East and Central Africa through improved regional trade and cross-border investment facilitation.

Education Transformation

Education remains the cornerstone of Uganda's development. Our commitment is to provide quality, accessible, and relevant education for all Ugandans.

Key Education Initiatives

Universal Education Enhancement

  • Maintain free Universal Primary Education (UPE)
  • Expand Universal Secondary Education (USE)
  • Introduce free technical and vocational training
  • Improve teacher training and welfare

Digital Learning Revolution

  • Equip all schools with computer laboratories
  • Provide high-speed internet connectivity
  • Develop digital learning content
  • Train teachers in ICT integration

Science and Technology Focus

Emphasize STEM education to produce the innovators and problem-solvers Uganda needs for its technological advancement and industrial development.

Healthcare System Strengthening

Achieving Universal Health Coverage and ensuring every Ugandan has access to quality, affordable healthcare services.

Health Targets by 2031

  • Reduce infant mortality to less than 25 per 1,000 live births
  • Increase life expectancy to 70 years
  • Achieve universal immunization coverage
  • Eliminate preventable diseases

Infrastructure Development

  • Build regional referral hospitals in every region
  • Establish health centers in every sub-county
  • Upgrade medical equipment and facilities
  • Expand specialized medical services

Human Resources for Health

Train and deploy adequate numbers of healthcare workers, improve their working conditions, and ensure equitable distribution across the country.

Infrastructure Development

Modern infrastructure is the backbone of economic development. We are committed to building world-class infrastructure to support Uganda's transformation.

Transport Network

Road Infrastructure

  • Complete the Standard Gauge Railway project
  • Expand and maintain the national road network
  • Construct bypass roads in major cities
  • Improve rural road connectivity

Energy Sector

  • Increase electricity access to 80% of households
  • Develop renewable energy sources
  • Expand the national grid
  • Promote energy efficiency programs

Water and Sanitation

Ensure universal access to clean, safe water and improved sanitation facilities for all Ugandans, with special focus on rural areas.

Agricultural Transformation

Agriculture employs over 70% of Ugandans. Our focus is on modernizing agriculture and moving from subsistence to commercial farming.

Modernization Strategy

  • Promote mechanization of agriculture
  • Improve access to quality inputs and seeds
  • Develop irrigation infrastructure
  • Strengthen agricultural extension services

Value Addition

  • Establish agro-processing facilities
  • Support farmer cooperatives
  • Develop agricultural markets
  • Improve post-harvest handling

Export Promotion

Position Uganda as a major food exporter to regional and international markets while ensuring food security at home.

Security & Defense

Maintaining peace, security, and stability is fundamental to Uganda's continued development and prosperity.

National Security

Defense Capabilities

  • Modernize the Uganda People's Defense Force (UPDF)
  • Strengthen border security
  • Combat terrorism and extremism
  • Enhance intelligence capabilities

Internal Security

  • Community policing programs
  • Crime prevention initiatives
  • Youth engagement in security
  • Modern policing equipment and training

Regional Stability

Continue playing a leading role in promoting peace and stability in the East African region and the African continent.

  • Increased life expectancy from 43 to 63 years
  • Achieved 95% immunization coverage
  • Built over 3,000 health facilities

Infrastructure Development

  • Constructed over 20,000 kilometers of roads
  • Increased electricity generation to 1,400MW
  • Built Entebbe Airport expansion
  • Completed major hydropower projects (Bujagali, Karuma)
